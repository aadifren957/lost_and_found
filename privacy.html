Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Smart Lost & Found. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

2. Data We Collect

To provide our services, we collect certain personal and non-personal information, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, and profile information provided during signup or Google Auth.
  • Item Details: Information about lost or found items, including titles, categories, locations, and descriptions.
  • Images: Photos of items you upload to help the matching process.

3. How We Use Data

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To manage your account and secure your experience.
  • Smart Matching: Our algorithms analyze item details to find potential matches between lost and found items.
  • Communication: To notify you when a match is found or for account-related updates.

4. Data Protection Assurance

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your passwords are encrypted, and item images are stored securely. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Cookies Usage

We use essential cookies and local storage to maintain your login session and provide a seamless navigation experience. These are necessary for the core functionality of the website.
